Nablus city :

Nablus is a Palestinian city located in the northern West Bank
and is known for its historical and cultural heritage.
It was founded more than 2,000 years ago
and is famous for its traditional markets and famous sweets such as Nabulsi Kunafa.
The city includes many historical monuments, such as mosques, churches, and public baths.
Nablus plays an important role in Palestinian economic and political life and is distinguished by the beauty of its nature surrounded by mountains.
